Creator's Note

While designing a landing page for a client, I faced repeated dissatisfaction with the design. I started experimenting with various fonts from Google Fonts, I finally found one that made the client super happy.

That's when I understood impact Font's have on websites. However, the process of copying and testing fonts one by one was incredibly tedious.

To solve this, I built a Font Tester tool, making it easier to test and preview multiple fonts without switching or refreshing, saving significant time.

Remember most websites have 80% text, so make sure you pay attention to the fonts you use. It can play significant role.

I hope it saves you hours of effort.
- Paul
