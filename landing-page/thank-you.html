Use cases

Deciding fonts

Tired of checking if the font works for your website and brand? well easily test them on live website and pick the one that's the most impactful

Google fonts

Preview, test, and compare 1500+ Google Fonts on your website—no need to open Google Fonts. Get the perfect look and generate the CSS instantly.

Rebranding

Easily refresh your website by testing new fonts, even without a Figma file. This tool lets you preview different typography styles instantly for a fresh look.

More use cases

Demo fonts

Effortlessly involve your client in the design process and showcase how different fonts appear on their website.

Type designers

Test your custom fonts and check how they appear on different websites and find an ideal audience.

Workshops

Design and type workshops can leverage this tool to effortlessly show how different types affects the overall design and ascetics of the brand.
