Live preview

Easily preview fonts on any webpage. If your design and final output look different, Font Tester ensures an accurate, real-time preview. Instantly apply fonts, tweak styles, and see the results live.

FONT LIBRARY

Font tester comes with pre-installed 1500+ Google fonts, so you can test them easily without having to constantly switch between the Google fonts page and your development work

Custom fonts

Want to test a custom fancy font on your website? Simply upload it to Font Tester and preview it on any site instantly. The best part? Nothing is uploaded to the cloud—everything stays on your local machine.

Live Compare Fonts

Most people spend several hours testing fonts and finally giving up out of frustration. Font tester's Live compare allows you to instantly preview and compare multiple fonts at once avoiding you the frustration of testing them one by one.
